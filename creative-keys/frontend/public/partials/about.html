About Your Instructor

Hello! My name is Jalielle Curtis, and I am a passionate music educator with over 10 years of experience teaching piano and music theory. I hold a degree in Music Education from the University of Michigan, and I am dedicated to helping students of all ages discover the joy of music.

I believe that music is a universal language that can enrich our lives in countless ways. My teaching philosophy centers around creating a positive and engaging learning environment where students can explore their creativity and develop their musical skills.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, I am here to guide you on your musical journey. I look forward to sharing my love of music with you!

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to schedule a lesson!
